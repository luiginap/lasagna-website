Mister Lasagna was born in London in 2016. Over 10 years, it became a real-world validation of a product, a format and a system — not a pitch deck.

Iconic London locations. International franchise in South Korea. A pandemic that forced strategic reinvention, leading to Lasagneria Italiana — a premium casual format built on the same operational backbone.

We didn't start with investors. We started with a product, refined it under pressure, and built the system around what actually works. Now we're looking for a partner to accelerate what we've already proven we can build.
