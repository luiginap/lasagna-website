Our Story

The original
lasagna fastfood

Everything started in 2016 — a simple idea: authentic Italian lasagna, fast, affordable and without compromise. Mister Lasagna quickly became a go-to across London's most iconic locations and even expanded to South Korea.

After a reinvention, Mister Lasagna is back at Leicester Square — and Marylebone is coming soon. Same passion, same recipes, same Italian soul.
