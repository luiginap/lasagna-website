Our Story

Born in London,
driven by one passion

Everything started in London in 2016, when Mister Lasagna brought authentic Italian lasagna into a fast, modern format. A passion that quickly turned into a success across some of London's most iconic locations — and even reached South Korea.

After the pandemic, we embraced a new vision: Lasagneria Italiana — a more refined concept with table service, a richer menu, and a complete Italian dining experience. Today, our Chelsea and Notting Hill restaurants are the home of authentic Italian lasagna at its finest.
